Events and Activities

At Labaika Group of Schools, learning goes beyond the four walls of the classroom. We believe that a truly well-rounded education includes opportunities for students to explore their talents, develop social skills, and engage with their community. That’s why our school calendar is enriched with diverse events and activities designed to inspire, challenge, and nurture every learner.

Our events are carefully planned to align with our academic curriculum and Islamic values, ensuring that students grow in knowledge, character, and spirituality. From intellectual competitions to faith-based celebrations, every activity is an opportunity to reinforce discipline, teamwork, leadership, and moral uprightness.

Varieties of Events & Activities Our Students Participate In

The varieties of events and activities our students participate in include:

Academic Competitions

Throughout the school year, your child will have opportunities to challenge their mind in academic competitions such as Spelling Bees, Mathematics Challenges, Science Fairs, and Essay Contests — all designed to boost intellectual growth, sharpen problem-solving skills, and encourage a spirit of healthy competition.

Inter-Class Debate Competition

An Inter-Class Debate Competition was organized at our school to bring the students together from various classes to engage in a lively exchange of ideas.

Spelling Bee Competition

Our school recently organized a Spelling Bee Competition aiming to enhance students’ vocabulary, spelling skills, and confidence in public speaking.

Islamic & Moral Programs

Our strong Islamic foundation means that your child will also engage in meaningful Islamic and moral programs. These include Qur’an Recitation Competitions, Islamic Knowledge Quizzes, Ramadan Tafsir Sessions, Eid Celebrations, and Islamic Public Lectures — each one aimed at deepening their understanding of Islam, instilling good character, and strengthening their connection to Allah.

Qur’an Memorization

Our students actively participate in Qur’an memorization, cultivating spiritual discipline and a deeper connection to Islamic teachings.

Qur’an Recitation

Our students engage in Qur’an memorization, strengthening their faith and enhancing their understanding of Islamic principles through dedicated practice.

Sports & Physical Activities

We value physical well-being just as much as mental growth. That’s why our sports and physical activities — including annual inter-house sports competitions, football matches, athletics, and fitness programs — encourage teamwork, discipline, and a healthy lifestyle.

Football Competition

Our students enthusiastically participate in football competitions, promoting teamwork, discipline, physical fitness, and a passion for sports.

Table Tennis Competition

Our students eagerly participate in table tennis competitions, enthusiastically showcasing their agility, strategic thinking, and competitive spirit.

Excursions & Educational Trips

Because we know that real learning often happens outside the classroom, we organize excursions and educational trips to museums, historical sites, farms, industries, and other learning centers. These experiences broaden their horizons, provide hands-on learning opportunities, and connect theory with real-life applications.

Excursions to Popular Waterfalls

Our students went for excursions to the breathtaking Erin-Ijesha Waterfalls, where they explored its cascading seven layers, enjoyed the serene natural environment, engaged in a lot of fun.

Educational Trip to Olumo Rock

Our students embarked on an educational trip to Olumo Rock in Abeokuta, where they climbed the historic ancient rock formation, learned about its significance as a refuge for the Egba people.

Cultural & Creative Arts

To nurture creativity and self-expression, our cultural and creative arts programs give students the chance to participate in drama performances, debates, art exhibitions, and cultural day celebrations. These activities allow them to explore their talents while appreciating the beauty of different cultures and traditions.

Craft Making Competitions

Our students partake in Craft Making Competitions where they creatively engage in activities such as beadwork, tie and dye, paper craft, pottery, and weaving, showcasing their artistic skills.

Drama Presentations

Our students engage in Drama Presentations where they act out well-scripted plays, role-play historical and cultural stories, showcase moral lessons through stage performances.
