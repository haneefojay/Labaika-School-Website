At Labaika Schools, education is more than academics — it is the nurturing of character, culture, and faith. Founded with a vision to raise well-rounded individuals, we combine the excellence of globally recognized education with a strong grounding in African cultural pride and the guiding principles of Islamic values.

We believe every child is unique, with talents and potential waiting to be discovered. Our role as educators is not only to teach but also to inspire, guide, and equip our students to thrive in today’s world while staying connected to their roots and faith.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that balances intellectual growth, moral development, and cultural identity, preparing students to become responsible global citizens with strong Islamic values.

Our Vission

To be a leading educational institution in Africa, known for academic excellence, cultural richness, and the nurturing of students who embody discipline, creativity, leadership, and compassion.

Our Values

Excellence: We hold ourselves to the highest standards in teaching and learning.

Faith: Islamic principles guide our environment, shaping character and conduct.

Culture: We embrace and celebrate African heritage as part of our identity.

Integrity: Honesty, respect, and responsibility are at the core of everything we do.

Community: We foster a supportive environment where students, parents, and staff grow together.
