Admissions Process

At Labaika Group of Schools, we open our doors to families who value a balanced education — one that blends academic excellence with strong Islamic values. Our admissions process is designed to be clear, transparent, and welcoming, ensuring that every prospective student and parent feels supported from the first inquiry to the first day of school.

We accept pupils and students into all levels of our school system, in line with our mission to nurture learners who are intellectually capable, morally upright, and spiritually grounded. Whether your child is joining us at the foundation stage or transferring from another school, we are committed to making their transition smooth and memorable.

We welcome admission applications from:

Pupils entering Playgroup, Nursery and Primary classes.

Students joining Junior and Senior Secondary classes.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Apply for Admission

We have made our admissions process simple and straightforward so that parents and guardians can enroll their children with ease. Here’s how to get started:

Step 1:

Initial Inquiry

Visit our school in person or contact our admissions office via phone or email to make any sort of enquiry:

08166626317, 08055609712, 08035266801

labaikaschools@gmail.com

Step 2:

Obtain an Admission Form

Purchase the Labaika Group of Schools Admission Form from the school’s administrative office.

Step 3:

Submission of Documents

Along with the completed admission form, submit the following documents:

Birth Certificate (or age declaration)

Two recent passport photographs

Immunization/Health Record

Step 4:

Entrance Assessment

Prospective students may be required to sit for an Entrance Test to assess their academic level in key subjects such as English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge. This helps us place students in the right class for their learning needs.

Step 5:

Interview Session

For certain classes, a short parent–student interview may be conducted to understand your child’s background, interests, and readiness for learning in a faith-based environment.

Step 6:

Offer of Admission

Successful applicants will receive an Admission Letter, along with details about school fees, uniform, textbooks, and resumption date. Note that the Admission Letter and other details will be sent to the Parent/Guardian WhatsApp Number or Email.

Step 7:

Payment of Fees

Parents should complete all required payments by a making a transfer of the school fees and all other necessary fees into the school bank account and submit proof of payment. A student file will then be created, and the student will be officially registered.

Step 8:

Orientation & Resumption

New students and parents are invited for an Orientation Program before the start of the term to familiarize themselves with our school environment, rules, and culture.

This entire Admission process step-by-step guide as shown that you have taken the right decision to enroll your child at Labaika Group of Schools.
